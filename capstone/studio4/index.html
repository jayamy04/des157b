Sudden Rise in Temperature of the Northern Atlantic Ocean

By Amy Lee

Scroll to Learn More

Did you know the temperature of the Northern Atlantic Ocean rose suddenly due to climate change in 2022 and 2023?
Let's look at this graph. The temperature in 2022 (in yellow) and 2023 (in red) are visibily higher than the previous years (in blue).
In the next 10-20 years, there will be lots of marine extinction and human deaths.
How can we prevent this? We can stop putting new carbon dioxide into the atmosphere and taking the old CO2 out of the atmosphere.
